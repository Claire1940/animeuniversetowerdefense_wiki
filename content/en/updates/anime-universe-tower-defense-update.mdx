### Expedition Loadout Point System

Unlike standard modes, the Expedition mode limits your loadout using a strict point-budget system. High-tier units consume significantly more points, forcing players to strategize and balance their teams carefully.

| Unit Rarity | Point Cost | Loadout Strategy |
|-|-|-|
| **Boundless & Higher** | 50 Points | Bring only one carry unit to anchor your team defense |
| **Secret** | 30 Points | Powerful alternative if you lack Boundless units |
| **Mythic** | 15 Points | Great for utility, farming, or secondary DPS |
| **Legendary** | 5 Points | Perfect for cheap buffs or early-game defense |
| **Epic** | 3 Points | Used to fill remaining slots if points are tight |

Navigating the Golden Palace requires patience and careful movement. Patrolling shadows cover the corridors; getting spotted fills their alert meter and drags you into disadvantageous combat.

### Stealth and Combat Rules

- **Cover Spots**: Blue indicators show designated hiding spots. Entering cover makes you completely invisible to patrols.
- **Dashing**: Use the dash mechanic to cross open corridors between cover spots without raising the alarm.
- **Preemptive Strikes**: Sneaking up behind an unaware shadow and initiating combat grants you the first turn and an immediate tactical advantage.
- **Locked Rooms**: Defeating guards yields keys. Use these keys to unlock sealed doors containing high-tier Arcana chests.

| Room Type | Key Required | Potential Rewards |
|-|-|-|
| **Standard Ambush** | No | Gold, basic EXP, and progression points |
| **Locked Room** | Yes | Arcana Chests (Magician, Lovers, Chariot) |
| **Elite Guard Room** | Yes | High-tier evolution materials and unit drops |
| **Boss Arena** | 10 Progression Points | Boundless Joker (0.1% base / 15% pity drop) |

<Callout type="tip" title="Progression Points">
You cannot enter the boss room immediately. You must defeat standard and elite enemies to accumulate **10 out of 10 progression points** before the boss arena gates will open.
</Callout>
