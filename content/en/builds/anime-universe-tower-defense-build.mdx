<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering the Mugetsu Transformation">

The **Final Moon Fang (Mugetsu)** form is Ichigo's trump card in this **anime universe tower defense build**. Activating this ability grants a 650% damage boost to the final attack and massive crit damage buffs. However, it comes with a severe drawback: once the form ends (after approximately 10 attacks), Ichigo reverts to his "Human Form," loses all upgrades, and becomes unsellable for 30 seconds.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Monitor Boss Progress">
    Wait until the boss has traversed at least 50% of Ichigo's range. Do not activate Mugetsu the moment the boss enters the circle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Check Support Buffs">
    Ensure your Virtual Idol or other support units have their active buffs ready. You want the Mugetsu attacks to be fully amplified.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Trigger Transformation">
    Activate the Final Moon Fang ability. Ichigo will transform and begin dealing massive burst damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Prepare for Reset">
    Once the 10 attacks are finished, Ichigo will reset to Upgrade 0. Have cash on hand to immediately re-upgrade him if the wave is still active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Advanced Tactic">
Using Mugetsu when the boss is near the exit of Ichigo's range is the most efficient method. Since Ichigo would be useless once the boss leaves his range anyway, the 30-second penalty doesn't hurt your defense.
</Callout>

</Section>
