<Section icon={Swords} title="Evolution and Etherealize Progression">

Progression in Universal Tower Defense Z doesn't stop at level 100. The Evolution and Etherealize systems allow players to push units to their absolute limits. Evolution usually requires specific materials found in Raids or Legend Stages, while Etherealization (E1 through E6) requires duplicate units or rare Shards.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Takedown Requirements">
    Most evolutions require between 5,500 and 7,500 takedowns. Equip the unit in a team and farm Infinite Mode on Easy/Normal to stack kills quickly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Collect Specific Materials">
    Identify the required boss drops. For example, Jinoo (Monarch) requires Shadow Tokens, while Ichiko (Rage) requires Hollow Essence from the Hueco Castle Raid.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Gather Universal Fragments">
    Universal Fragments are the common currency for all evolutions. These can be farmed in most Story stages or purchased in the Raid Shop.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Evolution at NPC">
    Visit the Evolution NPC in the lobby with all materials in your inventory. Ensure you are evolving the version of the unit with the best stats/traits.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Etherealization Priority">
Always prioritize Etherealizing your Main Carry to E6 first. The stat jump from E5 to E6 is significantly higher than the jump from E1 to E2, providing a massive power spike for Raid content.
</Callout>
