<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Quest Book Walkthrough">

To unlock the boss fights and earn the right to extract, you must complete the Quest Book objectives. If you ignore these quests, you will not receive any unit drops at the end of the run.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete Pizza Deliveries First">
    Locate the blue pizza icons on your map. Complete **5 pizza deliveries** immediately. This objective is shared across multiple unit quests and should be finished first to clear your map.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Hunt for Crime Scene Evidences">
    Listen for your Spider Senses to go off. Look through walls for glowing orbs: **3 Green Orbs** (for Green Goblin/Spider-Man quest) and **3 Orange/Blue Orbs** (for Iron Man quest). These are usually hidden on rooftops or behind alleyways.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Clear Red Exclamation Points">
    Trigger the red exclamation points to start 5-wave tower defense mini-games. You must clear these to collect specific enemy modifiers (such as Sprinter, Hyper Armor, Air, Cloner, and Range). Plan to clear **6 to 7 red zones** per run to satisfy all modifier requirements.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Physics & Collision Bugs">
The movement mechanics in this mode can be wonky. If you swing into walls or rooftops at maximum velocity, you may clip through the map, fall to your death, and instantly fail the run. **Always lose your momentum** before hitting solid objects.
</Callout>

### Boss Setup Guide

For all three bosses, place your money farms (Bulma) and max them out immediately using your carried-over cash. Position your rewind units (Moto) right at the entrance of the tracks to keep the bosses trapped in your main DPS range. 

Use Sailor King for critical hit buffs and ensure Nami is placed within Gogeta's range to activate his passive damage multipliers. When Venom or Tangerine's shields break, trigger Gogeta's ultimate nuke to chunk their health pools.
