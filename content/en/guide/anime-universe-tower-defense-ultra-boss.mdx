<Section icon={Map} title="How to Obtain and Farm Struggle Keys">

Before you can face the **anime universe tower defense ultra boss**, you must farm the Legend stages. The Kitsunox Struggle keys are dropped specifically from the Naruto Legend stage after completing the base story mode.

The drop rates are tiered based on your performance and difficulty settings. By default, the chance is approximately 5%, but this can be boosted significantly. Dedicated players often farm these keys during 2x drop events or by using luck-boosting items to reach the 25% cap. This makes the grind much more manageable when preparing for long-term AFK sessions.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Clear Story Mode">
    You must complete the entire Ninja Forest story arc before the Legend stages become accessible.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Enter Legend Mode">
    Navigate to the Legend Mode portal and select the Ninja Forest stage. Ensure you are geared for high-speed clears.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Increase Drop Luck">
    Use luck potions or specific unit passives to increase the key drop rate from the base 5% up to the 25% maximum.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Farm Tiered Keys">
    Collect Tier 1 through Tier 4 keys. Note that a single macro can often work for all tiers if your units are strong enough.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Tip">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Step-by-Step AFK Macro Strategy">

The most efficient way to handle the **anime universe tower defense ultra boss** is through an AFK macro. This allows you to farm the 18,000+ currency needed for the 200 trade re-rolls without active play. The key to a successful macro is "spam placement"—repeatedly clicking the placement area to account for potential lag or frame drops.

When setting up your macro, the order of operations is critical. You must establish your economy first but transition to defense quickly enough to catch the first wave of the four-path layout. Using the "Unit Manager" feature in-game is highly recommended to automate upgrades once units are placed.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initialize Farms">
    Instantly place both Speedwagon and Bulma. Set the macro to spam the placement button to ensure they land even during lag.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Deploy Early Defense">
    Place Ace or Virgil in a central location that covers at least three paths. If using Ace, ensure he is placed where he can hit the most enemies to build his range stacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Set Up Boss Killers">
    Place Zoro and Whitebeard near the end of the paths or in a high-traffic area. Do this before maxing out your farm units to ensure you have a safety net.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Automate Upgrades">
    Configure the Unit Manager to auto-upgrade Speedwagon first, followed by Bulma, then your primary DPS units.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Stock Management">
During the AFK run, the boss will use "Tailed Beast Bombs." In an AFK setup, you will likely lose one stock (life) during the final phase. This is normal and will not fail the run as long as your DPS is high enough to kill the boss afterward.
</Callout>

| Macro Action | Priority | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
