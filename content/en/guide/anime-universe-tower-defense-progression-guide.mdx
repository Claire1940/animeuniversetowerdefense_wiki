<Section icon={Shield} title="Endgame Content: Relics and Raids">

The final frontier of the **anime universe tower defense progression guide** involves the Relic system. Relics are equippable items that provide massive stat boosts to your units. However, they should only be a focus once you have a stable team of evolved meta units.

When building relics, you must balance three core stats: **Damage**, **Cooldown (CDR)**, and **Range**. For units with long base cooldowns like **Gojo**, investing heavily into Cooldown is often better than pure Damage, as it allows for more frequent stuns and ability uptime.

| Relic Focus | Recommended Stats | Best Units |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pure Power** | Damage / Crit Rate | Luffy, Zoro, Kaido |
| **Utility/Stun** | Cooldown / Range | Gojo, Sukuna |
| **Economy Boost** | Cost Reduction | Speedwagon, Bulma |
