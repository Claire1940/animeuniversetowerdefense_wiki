<Section icon={Swords} title="Rukia Ice Queen: Abilities and Bankai Mechanics">

Once evolved into the **anime universe tower defense rukia** Ice Queen form, she gains access to some of the most sophisticated crowd-control mechanics in the game. Her kit revolves around freezing enemies and applying debuffs that amplify damage for your entire team.

The most critical aspect of her gameplay is the **Ice Release (Bankai)**. When activated, Rukia transforms, gaining a massive 75% damage increase and a 30% reduction in SPA (Seconds Per Attack). However, this comes with a "Temperature" mechanic. If her temperature reaches max, she reverts to base form and becomes stunned for 10 seconds.

| Ability | Effect | Duration / Buff |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ice Spike** | Summons spike dealing 50% extra damage | Instant |
| **Cold as Ice** | Applies Freeze and 30% Slow | 4 Seconds |
| **Ice Release** | Bankai transformation form | Until Max Temp |
| **Shatter** | Deals 40% instant damage to frozen targets | Triggered |

<Callout type="warning" title="Temperature Management">
During Bankai, Rukia takes 4% more damage for every 10% increase in her temperature gauge. Watch the meter closely to avoid the 10-second stun penalty.
</Callout>

Farming the necessary materials for **anime universe tower defense rukia** requires consistent participation in the Hollowed Eclipse and Reaper City raids. The Frozen Sword is the bottleneck for most players, but it can be bypassed with enough dedication to the Raid Shop.

  <Step num="1" title="Obtain Base Rukia">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Craft Universal Fragments">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Clear Raid Act 4">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Evolution">

  </Step>
</Steps>
