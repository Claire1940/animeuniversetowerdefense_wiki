<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Evolution Path and Passive Enhancements">

To unlock the true potential of **anime universe tower defense ace**, evolving the unit is mandatory. Each evolution tier (E1 through E6) provides incremental buffs, but the most significant power spikes occur at E4 and E6. These passives fundamentally change how his burn damage interacts with enemy armor and health pools.

| Evolution Tier | Primary Buff Description | Impact on Gameplay |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **E2** | Increased burn damage to armored enemies | Essential for clearing tanky wave units |
| **E4** | Increases passive damage by 30% | Massive boost to overall DOT ticks |
| **E6** | Burn damage increases to 100% of total damage | Transforms Ace into a top-tier boss killer |

The jump from E5 to E6 is the most critical. At lower tiers, his burn damage only accounts for approximately 60% of his total damage. Reaching E6 allows his fire damage to match his total damage output 1:1, effectively doubling his effectiveness against bosses that stay within his range for extended periods.

<Callout type="warning" title="Evolution Requirement">
