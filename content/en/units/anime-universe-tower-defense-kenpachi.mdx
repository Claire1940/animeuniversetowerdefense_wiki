<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Evolution and Farming Guide">

To unleash the full power of your **anime universe tower defense kenpachi**, you must evolve him. Evolution drastically increases his base stats and changes his visual appearance to a much cooler aesthetic. The key evolution material required is the **Eye Patch**, which drops from specific Legend Stages.

### Legend Stage Drop Rates

| Stage Name | Difficulty | Eye Patch Drop Rate | Holy Fragment Drop Rate | Blue Star Drop Rate |
|--- |--- |--- |--- |--- |
| Hollowed Moon Act 2 | Normal | 10% | 0% | 18% |
| Hollowed Moon Act 3 | Normal | 10% | 10% | 10% |
| Hollowed Moon Act 3 | Hard | Increased | Increased | Increased |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access Legend Stages">
    Navigate to the Story Mode portal and select the **Legend Stages** menu.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Hollowed Moon">
    Choose **Hollowed Moon Act 2** or **Act 3**. Act 2 has a higher drop rate for Blue Stars, while Act 3 offers a balanced drop rate for both the Eye Patch and Holy Fragments.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Farm the Eye Patch">
    Run the stage repeatedly. On average, it takes about 10 runs to secure the Eye Patch drop. You can increase the difficulty to Hard for better drop rates if your team is strong enough.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Evolve at the Police Station">
    Head over to the upgrade section at the **Police Station**, talk to the Evolution NPC, and use your Eye Patch along with supporting fragments to complete the evolution.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Farming Efficiency">
Do not waste time on lower acts if you can handle Act 3. Act 3 provides the most efficient resource loop for both Kenpachi's evolution and general upgrade materials.
</Callout>

</Section>

### Stat Points & Etherealization
When distributing stat points, do not dump everything into damage. While damage is the priority, a balanced point spread across cooldown and range ensures Kenpachi does not let fast enemies slip past. Additionally, work on **Etherealization** whenever he is featured on banners. At **Etherealization Level 6 (Cut Through Everything)**, Kenpachi gains an additional **20% elemental damage**.

<Callout type="success" title="Stat Lock Recommendation">
If you roll an SS rank on Cooldown or Range, use a **Stat Lock** before rerolling your other stats. This ensures you do not lose a perfect roll while hunting for that elusive SS Damage.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Strategic Synergies and Meiku Setup">

To push Kenpachi's damage to astronomical numbers, you must utilize support units. The absolute best support companion for him is **Meiku**. 

### Support Synergy Mechanics

| Support Unit | Buff Type | Interaction with Kenpachi | Placement Strategy |
|--- |--- |--- |--- |
| **Meiku** | Damage / Berserker | Triggers Berserker state for massive DPS | Place directly adjacent to Kenpachi |
| **Buffer Units** | Range / Cooldown | Increases coverage and attack speed | Place within active range of Kenpachi |

### How to Execute the Meiku Setup
1. **Place Kenpachi** at your primary choke point.
2. **Place Meiku** directly adjacent to him so her buff radius completely covers his placement circle.
3. **Upgrade Meiku** and switch her active buff mode to **Damage Buff**.
4. This activates the **Berserker** status on Kenpachi, multiplying his already massive Ruler trait damage.

<Callout type="info" title="Relic Farming Tip">
Farm Hard Mode stages to secure a **Mythic Damage Relic**. Equipping this on your evolved Kenpachi, combined with Meiku's active buff, allows you to easily solo early to mid-game infinite runs for gem grinding.
</Callout>
