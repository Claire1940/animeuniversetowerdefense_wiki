<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering the Mugetsu Ability">

The core mechanic of **anime universe tower defense ichigo** is the "Final Moon Fang" (Mugetsu) ability. When activated, Ichigo enters a temporary state of absolute power, but this comes with a heavy cost. Understanding the cycle of this ability is the difference between a successful raid and a total defeat.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Activation Phase">
    Trigger the active ability when the boss or a large wave enters Ichigo's range. He gains a 250% damage buff and a 50% crit rate boost immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Mugetsu Strike">
    Ichigo releases a massive area-of-effect attack dealing 650% of his current damage. This hit usually clears all non-boss enemies instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Decay Phase">
    Each attack following the strike reduces his buffs by 10%. Once the buffs reach zero, he enters "Human Form."
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Penalty Period">
    In Human Form, Ichigo is unsellable and loses all upgrades for 30 seconds. After this, he reverts to his base placement form.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Ability State | Damage Multiplier | Crit Chance | Special Effects |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Base Form** | 100% | 46% | Stun Counter, Pressure |
| **Mugetsu Form** | 250% | 96% | 650% AoE Strike |
| **Human Form** | 0% | 0% | Unsellable, No Upgrades |

<Callout type="info" title="Strategy Note">
If you have surplus gold, sell Ichigo the moment he enters Human Form. You can then re-place him and max him out immediately to reset the ability cooldown.
</Callout>
