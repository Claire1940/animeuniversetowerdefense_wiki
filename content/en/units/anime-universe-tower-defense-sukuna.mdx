Automating your runs for the **anime universe tower defense sukuna** portal saves hours of manual farming. Below is the proven step-by-step macro setup using a standard team of Sinbad, Frieren, Kenpachi, Miku, and double farms (Speedwagon/Bulma).

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Placement">
    Start your macro recording. Place Speedwagon immediately to begin generating income. Place Frieren near the main pathway loop, ensuring the edge of her attack circle covers the corner. Place Kenpachi nearby to handle early leaks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Economy & Support Setup">
    Enable the auto-upgrade feature on your macro. Place Bulma and upgrade her to maximum level. Once your economy is secured, place Sinbad in the main damage zone and place Miku just behind your frontline.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="DPS Upgrades & Skill Settings">
    Set Frieren to her **Utility** track to enable constant stuns on passing enemies. Auto-upgrade Sinbad and Kenpachi to their maximum levels. Finally, set Miku to her damage track and turn on auto-skills to automate buffs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Boss Phase Brute Force">
    When the boss spawns, ignore the interactive flame mechanics. Your Frieren's stuns and Kenpachi's slow will keep the boss locked in the primary kill zone. Allow your high-DPS units to brute force through the healing phases until victorious.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Macro Optimization">
Always use the 'auto-upgrade' option in your macro software rather than recording individual click coordinates. If your team takes slightly longer to kill a wave, a coordinate-based click might execute before you have enough cash, breaking the entire macro sequence.
</Callout>
