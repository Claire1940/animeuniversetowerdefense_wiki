<Section icon={Trophy} title="Evolution Path and Material Requirements">

Evolving Jinoo to his final form, the **Shadow Monarch**, is a significant investment that requires multiple rare materials and thousands of takedowns. The process is split into two distinct tiers: the Monarch evolution and the ultimate Shadow Monarch ascension. In **Anime Universe Tower Defense**, players must balance their resource spending between summoning new Update 4 units and maxing out established carries like Jinoo.

The first evolution, **Jinoo (Monarch)**, serves as a bridge. It improves his base stats and changes his attack pattern to include wider area-of-effect (AoE) damage. The second stage, **Jinoo, Shadow Monarch**, is where the unit reaches its true potential, gaining massive stat boosts and the ability to hit both ground and air targets effectively.

| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

<Callout type="warning" title="Important">
The **Boundless Orb** is an extremely rare drop from the Universal Rift. Do not use this material on secondary units until you have secured Jinoo's final evolution, as it is the primary bottleneck for endgame progression.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Evolution Guide">

To successfully evolve Jinoo to his peak performance, follow this structured progression route. This ensures you do not waste Gems or Fragments on unnecessary upgrades.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Base Jinoo">
    Obtain the base Jinoo unit through the standard summoning banner. Use your Gems during high-rate events or use Luck Potions to increase your chances of pulling this Mythic-tier unit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm Takedowns in Infinite Mode">
    Equip Jinoo in your primary team and enter Infinite Mode on a lower difficulty. Focus all your early-wave upgrades on Jinoo to ensure he gets the final blow on enemies. You need 7,500 takedowns for the first evolution.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Collect Shadow Tokens and Fragments">
    Participate in Shadow-themed progression stages and Raids to collect Shadow Tokens. Universal Fragments can be obtained through daily challenges or by dismantling duplicate high-rarity units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Monarch Evolution">
    Visit the Evolution NPC in the lobby once you have met the takedown and material requirements. This will transform Jinoo into his Monarch form, increasing his base damage significantly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Ascend to Shadow Monarch">
    Gather the final set of materials, including the rare Boundless Orb and unique boss drops like the Ant King Egg or Shadow Knight Sword. Complete the final evolution to unlock the single-placement Shadow Monarch.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Evolution Complete">
Once Jinoo reaches the **Shadow Monarch** stage, he becomes a "Single Placement" unit. This means he takes up only one slot on the field but provides damage equivalent to five standard units.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Best Traits and Stat Rerolls for Jinoo">

In **Anime Universe Tower Defense**, traits can make or break a unit's viability. For Jinoo, you should aim for traits that either amplify his raw damage or reduce his attack cooldown. Since the Shadow Monarch form is limited to a single placement, traits with placement penalties (like Ruler) are actually beneficial because they provide massive buffs without any real downside for this specific unit.

The **Ruler** trait is widely considered the "God Tier" for Jinoo. It provides a 200% damage increase and reduces cooldowns by 20%. Since Jinoo is already limited to one placement in his final form, the "Limits to 1 placement" drawback of the Ruler trait is completely negated.

| Trait Name | Roll Chance | Damage Buff | Cooldown Red. | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ruler** | 0.1% | **+200%** | -20% | Boss Killing / General DPS |
| **Eternal** | 0.2% | +5%/wave | -20% | Infinite Mode (Late Waves) |
| **Fission** | 0.15% | +15% | -15% | Radiation DoT Stacking |
| **Sacred** | 0.3% | +25% | -10% | All-purpose/Early Game |

<Callout type="info" title="Stat Optimization">
When using Stat Rerolls, focus on achieving an **S-Tier** or **Z-Tier** grade in **Damage** and **SPA (Seconds Per Attack)**. Range is secondary for Jinoo, as his Shadow Monarch form already possesses one of the largest attack radii in the game.
</Callout>
