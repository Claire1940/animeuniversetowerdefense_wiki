The final evolution requires four rare fragments scattered across different end-game modes.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Passives and Haki Mechanics">

The true power of **anime universe tower defense zoro** lies in his complex passive abilities and his unique King of Hell Haki system. When managed correctly, his damage output rivals any unit in the current meta.

| Passive Name | Mechanic | Effect |
|--- |--- |--- |
| **King of Hell** | Active Ability | Consumes 10 Haki per attack; grants **+50% Damage** and **+100% Crit Rate** |
| **Pirate Hunter** | Boss Targeting | Halves AoE size but grants **+50% Damage** and **+75% Armor Damage** against bosses |
| **Color of Armaments** | Haki Regen | Generates **+1 Haki** per active bleed tick on enemies |
| **Brutal Slashes** | Bleed DOT | Deals **85% critical bleed damage** over 7 seconds; triggers **75% follow-up damage** |

<Callout type="info" title="Haki Depletion Penalty">
If Zoro's Haki drops below 10, the King of Hell ability deactivates, stunning Zoro for 10 seconds and disabling his Pirate Hunter passive for 30 seconds. Deactivate the ability manually before Haki runs out to avoid this penalty.
</Callout>
