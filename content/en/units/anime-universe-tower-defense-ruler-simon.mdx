<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Mech Control">

Ruler Simon is not a typical passive tower defense unit. Upon placement, he functions as a hybrid summoner. Instead of attacking directly in his base form, he summons his massive drill mech onto the nearest path to block and shred incoming enemies.

| Skill Name | Damage Type | Target Type | Special Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Mech Summon** | Physical | Ground | Places the drill mech on the path |
| **Missile Drill Barrage** | Physical/Explosive | Full AoE | Fires multiple tracking projectiles |
| **Pierce the Stars** | Elemental | Full AoE | Triggers cinematic cutscene, massive damage |

When you activate his **Mech Control** ability, the gameplay shifts completely. You gain manual control over the mech for 120 seconds, allowing you to move freely across the map and execute powerful active abilities.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Place the Unit">
    Deploy Simon on a strategic high-ground location. He is expensive to place, so ensure your economy is established first.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Charge the Passive">
    Allow the mech to attack automatically to build up his charge meter. Higher charge increases his overall AoE size.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Activate Mech Control">
    Trigger the active ability to pilot the mech manually. Use this mode to reposition the mech directly in front of fast-moving bosses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Pierce the Stars">
    Once fully charged in manual mode, unleash his ultimate skill to trigger his signature cinematic and deal massive full-map damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>
