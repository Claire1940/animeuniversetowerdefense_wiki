<Section icon={Target} title="Moonless Sky Raid Strategy">

The Moonless Sky game mode is the ultimate testing ground for your units. It features enemies with high hyper armor and massive health pools. Successfully soloing this mode requires a specific placement strategy and careful management of your farm units.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Early Game Setup">
    Place your money farms immediately. Use a cheap unit like Vegito or a base-level Ichigo to handle the first few waves. Focus on auto-upgrading farms to build a massive economy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Hybrid Conversion">
    Upgrade Ichigo to his fourth level as quickly as possible. This converts him into a hybrid unit, allowing him to target both ground and air enemies, which is vital for mid-game waves.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Support Deployment">
    Place an Idol and Nami. The Idol provides a necessary damage track buff, while Nami (or Zombie units) handles the shields that Ichigo might struggle to penetrate quickly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Final Boss Cycle">
    When the boss spawns, activate "Final Moon Fang." Once the form ends and Ichigo enters his human form, sell him and replace him immediately to reset his ability cooldown.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Pro Strategy">
The active ability cooldown is not global. If you have enough cash, selling and replacing your main DPS is the fastest way to keep the "Final Moon Fang" buff active throughout the boss fight.
</Callout>
