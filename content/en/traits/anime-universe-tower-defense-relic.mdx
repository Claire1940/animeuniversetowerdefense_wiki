<Section icon={Swords} title="Case Study: Ichigo Mugetsu Build">

Using the **anime universe tower defense relic** system on a top-tier unit like Ichigo reveals the true power of the Mugetsu form. This form, also known as Final Moon Fang, grants a massive 650% damage boost but comes with a severe drawback: the unit eventually reverts to a "Human Form" and becomes unsellable and useless for 30 seconds.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Trait Selection">
    Roll for the **Ruler** trait. It provides the highest raw damage boost required to make the most of the Shadow Reaper relic multipliers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Relic Alignment">
    Equip the full Shadow Reaper set. Ensure the Necklace has **Elemental Damage** that matches Ichigo's affinity to maximize the global amp.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Ability Timing">
    Do not use Mugetsu immediately. Wait until the boss is at the mid-point of your range. This ensures that by the time you enter the 30-second penalty phase, the boss has already exited your range.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade Management">
    Mugetsu resets upgrades to level zero after the duration ends. Ensure you have enough cash reserves to instantly re-upgrade the unit once the Human Form penalty expires.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Strategy">
Ichigo's follow-up attacks increase in duration for every enemy killed. This makes him significantly stronger against waves of minions than against a lone boss. Use him as a "gatekeeper" to clear the path for your single-target specialists.
