Every unit in Anime Universe Tower Defense features three distinct slots for relic customization: Top, Bottom, and Accessory (Hat). The Top and Bottom slots function as a cohesive set. Equipping matching pieces from the same set unlocks powerful passive bonuses that can alter your combat performance.

| Relic Set Name | Set Pieces Required | Active Set Bonus Effects | Elemental Affinity |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Junior Ninja** | Jacket + Pants | Damage +10%, Wind Damage +10% | Wind |
| **Master Ninja** | Coat + Pants | Damage +5%, Fire/Dark Rose Damage +10% | Fire, Dark Rose |
| **Laughing Captain** | Coat + Pants | Damage +5%, Range +5%, Attack Cooldown -5% | None (Universal) |
| **Sun God** | Shirt + Pants | Damage +5%, Light/Water/Ice Damage +10% | Light, Water, Ice |
| **Berserk Shinigami** | Uniform + Pants | Damage +5%, Armor Damage +15% | Physical / Armor Break |
| **X Captain Uraro** | Coat + Pants | Critical Rate +10%, Critical Damage +25% | Critical Focus |

Unlike the Top and Bottom slots, the Accessory slot operates independently. Accessories do not require a set partner to activate their unique perks. Instead, each individual accessory carries its own powerful intrinsic effect.

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Farm Mythic Relics and Accessories">

Farming Mythic relics efficiently requires utilizing different game modes based on the specific slot you want to target. Top and Bottom pieces are obtained through Story Mode and the AFK Zone, while high-end Accessories are exclusive to the Virtual Realm.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Clear Nightmare Story Mode">
    Complete the highest difficulty of your target map to unlock the best possible drop rates. Nightmare mode is highly accessible once you evolve your first DPS unit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Optimize AFK Zone Speeds">
    Your fastest clear time on Story Mode stages dictates your reward interval in the AFK Zone. Clear Nightmare stages as quickly as possible to reduce your AFK reward timer.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Conquer the Virtual Realm">
    Form a coordinated team to clear the Virtual Realm. This challenging game mode is the sole source of Mythic Hats and exclusive utility accessories.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Map Drops">
Remember that specific maps drop themed sets. Farm Ninja Forest for Naruto-themed sets, Marine Base for One Piece-themed sets, and Hollow Moon for Bleach-themed sets.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimizing Perks for DPS and Support Units">

When rolling and upgrading your relics, your primary focus should be on the main perk. While side perks offer nice minor bonuses, they are heavily subject to RNG and can be difficult to optimize.

| Unit Archetype | Recommended Accessory Perk | Recommended Top/Bottom Perk | Performance Impact |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Pure DPS** (e.g., Jinu, Ace) | Pure Damage | Pure Damage | Up to +120% total damage scaling |
| **Buff Supports** (e.g., Virtual Idol) | **Buff Potency** | Range / Cooldown | Maximizes active stat multipliers |
| **DoT Specialists** (e.g., Fire/Bleed) | Pure Damage | Damage / DoT Amp | Scaled directly off base damage stats |

<Callout type="info" title="DoT Calculation Rule">
Do not prioritize Damage Over Time (DoT) perks over flat Damage perks on characters like Ace. Because burn and bleed damage scale directly off the unit's base attack damage, increasing flat Damage yields superior overall performance.
</Callout>
