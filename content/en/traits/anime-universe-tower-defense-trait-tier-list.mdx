<Section icon={Swords} title="A-Tier & B-Tier Traits: Strong & Reliable Options">

A-Tier and B-Tier traits are highly competitive and serve as excellent end-game options if you run out of reroll resources. They offer specialized bonuses that shine when paired with the right character kits.

### A-Tier: Specialized Excellence
These traits require specific unit types to unlock their full potential but perform almost on par with S-Tier when optimized.

- **Fish (0.15%):** Adds a unique radiation damage-over-time effect alongside solid stat boosts. Excellent on rapid-fire attackers.
- **Wizard (0.5%):** The premier choice for burn or poison units, boosting DOT damage by a massive 30%.
- **Duelist (0.8%):** A dedicated boss killer. The 35% extra damage against boss units makes it invaluable for late-game stages.
- **Artifiser (0.65%):** Scales hard in the late game once you have fully invested in high-tier relics.

### B-Tier: Solid Backbones
These traits are reliable and provide noticeable upgrades to your standard units.

| Trait | Best Used On | Why It Works |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dead Eye** | Snipers & Beam Units | 20% Range allows safer placements and early map coverage. |
| **Fortunate** | Money Farms & Support | 20% Income boost snowballs economy in long matches. |
| **Lethal** | Area-of-Effect (AoE) Units | 30% finisher damage helps clean up leaking enemies. |
| **Light Speed** | Ability-Heavy Units | 20% Cooldown reduction keeps active skills running constantly. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Endgame Warning">
Do not settle for B-Tier traits on your primary DPS units. While they are great for mid-game progression, you will need the raw power of S-Tier or A-Tier traits to clear late-game challenges.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Optimize and Roll Your Traits">

Getting the perfect setup requires understanding when to keep a roll and when to discard it. Follow this strategic progression path to maximize your resources.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify Unit Archetypes">
    Determine if your unit is a raw DPS, a DOT applicator, or an economy support. Never roll for damage traits on pure economy units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Settle on Mid-Tier Holders">
    During early progression, keep traits like **Agile**, **Lethal**, or **Dead Eye**. These offer enough utility to clear story chapters without wasting tokens.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Target Specialized A-Tiers">
    If you roll a trait like **Wizard** on a burn unit, stop rolling. Matching specialized traits to the correct unit kit is often cheaper than chasing S-Tiers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Go All-In on S-Tier for Main DPS">
    Save your bulk reroll tokens for your primary damage dealers. Target **Ruler** or **Sacred** to maximize your team's damage ceiling.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Economy Strategy">
Use **Fortunate** on your support units early in a match. The extra cash flow allows you to upgrade your S-Tier DPS units much faster.
</Callout>
